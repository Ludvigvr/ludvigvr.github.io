Welcome to Vietnam. The land of the Ascending Dragon

Vietnam is a great nation, full of strong, hardworking, and patriotic people. From the ancient kingdoms to the modern republic, Vietnam has always been a land of culture, tradition, and innovation. Vietnam's soldiers have fought valiantly to defend their country's freedom and sovereignty. Today, Vietnam is a thriving nation and is poised to take its place among the world's great powers. As Vietnamese, we must always remember our country's success is our own success, and we must be proud of our heritage, culture, and achievements. Let us raise our heads high and proclaim our pride in Vietnam, the land of the rising dragon!
